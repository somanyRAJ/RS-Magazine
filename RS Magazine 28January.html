January 28, 2024

The NEEV 2024 competition organized by the CEST Club at NITR saw a remarkable display of talent and innovation, with teams from various engineering disciplines competing fiercely for top honors. Among these trailblazing teams was one comprised of civil engineer Mr. Raghuraj Somani, mechanical engineer Ms. Pankaj Kumawat, mechanical engineer Mr. Nikhil Rajpurohit, and ECE engineer Mr. Shyamsunder Tard. Their collaborative efforts not only showcased their diverse expertise but also culminated in a remarkable victory that left a lasting impression on the competition.

In the Setu - the bridge building competition, Mr. Somani's expertise in civil engineering combined seamlessly with the mechanical prowess of Ms. Kumawat and Mr. Rajpurohit. Their meticulously designed and precisely executed bridge not only withstood rigorous testing but also stood out for its innovative design and structural integrity, earning them the top spot and a well-deserved trophy.

Following their success in the bridge building challenge, the team shifted gears to Gatisheel - the roller coaster making competition. Leveraging their collective knowledge of mechanical engineering principles, the team crafted a roller coaster that not only thrilled spectators but also impressed the judges with its flawless execution and attention to detail. Their outstanding performance secured them another first prize and trophy, further solidifying their position as frontrunners in the competition.

The 1st Prize Trophee(s) and certificates was handed over to the team led by Mr. Somani at the ceremony.

Somani's success underscores the importance of interdisciplinary collaboration and creative problem-solving in addressing global challenges. As the world grapples with the twin crises of food insecurity and environmental degradation, his victory serves as a beacon of hope, reminding us that with determination and innovation, we can overcome even the most daunting obstacles.

Despite facing stiff competition in the civil engineering quiz, the team's comprehensive understanding of their respective fields and collaborative spirit helped them secure a respectable fourth position. However, it was their consistent performance across multiple challenges that ultimately propelled them to victory, clinching the top spot in the overall standings.

For Mr. Somani, Ms. Kumawat, Mr. Rajpurohit, and Mr. Tard, winning the NEEV 2024 competition wasn't just a testament to their individual skills, but also a testament to the power of teamwork, innovation, and interdisciplinary collaboration. As they proudly hoisted the trophy, they not only celebrated their own success but also served as an inspiration to aspiring engineers everywhere, proving that with determination, creativity, and teamwork, anything is possible.
