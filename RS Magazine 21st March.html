1st Runner's Up Of
'EV pe Charcha' Debate

Organized by Sustainable Development Goals Campus Club,
National Institute of Technology-Rourkela

Starring Raghuraj Somani

March 21, 2024

Raghuraj Somani, a student hailing from the esteemed National Institute of Technology, Rourkela (NIT Rourkela), showcased his prowess in oration and debate by clinching the prestigious first runners-up position in the captivating discourse titled 'EV Pe Charcha.' Organized by the SDG Campus Club NITR, this engaging debate revolved around the contentious topic of "Fuel Cell Vehicles vs. Electric Vehicles."

With a fervent dedication to presenting a compelling argument, Raghuraj Somani advocated for the advancement of fuel cell vehicles, captivating the audience with his articulate discourse. Fuel cell vehicles, a promising alternative in the landscape of sustainable transportation, garnered significant attention as Raghuraj adeptly elucidated their potential benefits and advantages over electric vehicles.

Drawing upon his extensive research and understanding of the subject matter, Raghuraj skillfully articulated the merits of fuel cell vehicles, emphasizing their efficiency, environmental friendliness, and potential to revolutionize the automotive industry. His persuasive rhetoric resonated with the audience, sparking thoughtful discourse and contemplation on the future of transportation technology.

The 1st Runner's Up Trophee was handed over to Mr. Raghuraj at the ceremony.
