Champion Of
Case Study Competition

Organized by AFST(I)Rourkela Chapter and Food Tech Society in assosiation with Inquizzitive and SDG NITR

Starring Raghuraj Somani

October 16, 2023

In a resounding victory that resonated beyond the confines of a competition hall, Raghuraj Somani clinched the top prize at the prestigious case study competition organized by AFST(I) Rourkela Chapter and Food Tech Society in association with Inquizzitive and SDG NITR. The competition, held on the occasion of World Food Day on October 16, 2023, tackled one of the most pressing issues of our time: the disposal of food waste at both commercial and residential levels.

Somani's triumph was not just a personal achievement but a testament to his innovative approach and dedication to finding sustainable solutions. Faced with the complex problem statement, he delved deep into research, collaborated with experts, and crafted a comprehensive strategy that addressed the root causes of food waste while proposing actionable measures for mitigation.

The case study competition served as a platform for budding minds like Somani to showcase their ingenuity and commitment to making a positive impact on society. His winning solution not only impressed the judges but also sparked conversations and inspired others to rethink their approach to food waste management.

The 1st Prize Trophee was handed over to Mr. Raghuraj at the ceremony.
